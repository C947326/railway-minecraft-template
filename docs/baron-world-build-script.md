# Baron World Build Script

This is the direct in-game execution version of the Baron world-content pass. It is written to be followed while building in Minecraft.

## Part 1: Antenna Nest Build Script

Build the first complete hideout before doing anything elaborate elsewhere.

## Step 1: Pick The Exact Site

Use the configured `antenna_nest` area and stand where the Baron should be most visible but not instantly trapped.

Site criteria:

- elevated
- visible from a distance
- enough flat space for a `9x11` footprint
- one obvious outward-facing side for the lookout chair

## Step 2: Lay The Footprint

Create a `9x11` main shack footprint.

Suggested shape:

- `9` wide front face
- `11` deep body
- front platform protruding `2` blocks
- rear corner reserved for sleep alcove

Build materials:

- stripped spruce support posts
- dark oak floor and trim
- mixed stone brick and cracked stone brick lower walls

## Step 3: Build The Main Shell

Construct:

- walls to roughly `4` blocks high
- uneven roofline
- one open side or window slit facing the horizon
- one back corner partition for sleeping

Do not make it elegant. It should look expensive in taste but improvised in execution.

## Step 4: Add The Antenna Frame

On the roof or side platform, add:

- scaffolding tower or raised catwalk
- `4-6` lightning rods
- exposed copper blocks or stairs
- one chain-hung lantern

This silhouette matters. Players should know from a distance that the place is wrong.

## Step 5: Build The Front Watch Spot

On the outward-facing side, place:

- one chair or stair-and-sign seat
- one small side table or crate
- one lantern

This should feel like where the Baron drinks, watches, and lectures the air.

## Step 6: Place The Signal Desk

In the central interior, place:

- cartography table
- lectern
- item frame with compass
- map
- redstone dust detail
- repeater or comparator detail

This is the intellectual center of the hideout.

## Step 7: Place The Ledger Corner

In one side corner, place:

- `Ledger Barrel`
- one extra barrel
- loose paper or item frame
- one junk block or messy prop

Put the `Techtonic Asset Mobility Summary` book or paper here.

## Step 8: Place The Liquor Shelf

On one wall, place:

- shelf or barrel stack
- glass bottles
- potions
- smoker or campfire nearby if it helps the mood

Put `On Liquor, Noise, and Detection` here.

## Step 9: Build The Sleep Alcove

In the rear corner, place:

- bed or cot
- trapdoor partition
- one chest, satchel, or escape bag
- one dim lantern or candle

This should look used, not cozy.

## Step 10: Add The Burn Pile

Place:

- campfire or soul campfire
- charcoal-adjacent dark blocks
- one sign or frame implying destroyed papers

This should read as active evidence destruction, not decoration.

## Step 11: Add The Concealed Stash

Hide under trapdoor, under floor, or behind a panel:

- compass
- spyglass
- gunpowder
- `If I Leave In A Hurry`

This is for players who poke at everything.

## Step 12: Add Techtonic Evidence

Somewhere visible, place at least one of:

- `Techtonic Sponsor Read Draft`
- `Episode 34 Listener Numbers`
- `Techtonic Platinum Investor Certificate`
- a crate labeled or implied to be merch

The hideout must feel connected to the stolen podcast money, not merely paranoia.

## Step 13: Add Final Atmosphere

Before leaving, check for:

- uneven lantern placement
- one half-finished meal
- one bottle in an irresponsible place
- one chair with a strong sightline
- one point where the Baron can plausibly stand while speaking

## Part 3: Settlement Rumor Board Checklist

Every town and settlement should get a board. Do not wait for all seven hideouts to be built.

## Settlement Board Placement Rule

For each settlement, choose one of:

- well
- square
- dock
- gate
- church
- smithy
- inn exterior

Then place:

- one header sign
- one sighting sign
- one warning sign
- one comic sign
- optional witness sign

## Quick Placement Checklist

For each settlement:

- identify the highest-footfall location
- place the board where a player naturally pauses
- choose a unique header
- choose one sighting line
- choose one warning line
- choose one comic line
- add one witness line if the settlement feels rich enough
- avoid duplicating the exact same board in adjacent settlements

## Part 4: First Session Order Of Work

If you want to make visible progress in a single session:

1. build the shell of `antenna_nest`
2. place the desk, chair, lectern, and burn pile
3. enter Books 1, 2, and 3
4. stock the `Ledger Barrel`, `Techtonic Crate`, and stash
5. place rumor boards in the settlements you care about most
6. place one abandoned camp clue
7. place one Techtonic disposal box
8. spawn the Baron and test the encounter

That is the minimum viable world-fiction pass.
