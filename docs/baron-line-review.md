## Candidate Tone Shift

Target direction:

- more erratic
- more chemically frayed
- selectively vulgar
- more conspiratorial
- still articulate, not merely random profanity

The best lines should sound like a rich, sleep-deprived man who has been drinking, dosing, hiding, and over-explaining himself for too long, but who still speaks like an intelligent man.

They should also occasionally reference:

- Techtonic
- the stolen money
- investor language twisted into self-justification
- the Baron's insistence that he did not "steal" anything so much as "reallocate" it
